ABOUT ME

Hi there! I am Zein from good ol' Queens, New York. I am a scientist by profession and a web developer by hobby. I grauduated from CUNY John Jay College (lets go bloodhounds!). I have ate, breathed and slept in the "attractive" city of Gotham. It was just a few months ago that I became heavily passionate about programming. It started off with small doses of codecademy courses, but I quickly found myself overdosing on programming. So, I am still in my early trimester of programming. I have grown past the fetal stage and now I currently journey towards early childhood. *Note: This is a work under progress, so please excuse me for any defects, syntax errors, spelling errors, functional errors or any human errors period.
